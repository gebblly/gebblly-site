2. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, disclose, transfer, and protect personal information when you visit our website, use our mobile applications, place or receive orders, register as a customer, merchant, restaurant, or driver, contact support, participate in promotions, or otherwise interact with the Services.

This policy applies to the GEBBLLY platform and to any website, mobile application, landing page, onboarding flow, dashboard, or other digital interface that links to or references this Privacy Policy.

By using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly, information collected automatically when you use the Services, and information generated as part of order fulfillment and platform operations.

Information you may provide includes full name, phone number, email address, password and login credentials, delivery address, billing address, account preferences, merchant or business details, restaurant onboarding information, driver onboarding information, support requests, and other information you choose to provide.

Payments made through the platform are processed by third-party payment service providers, including Tranzila. GEBBLLY does not store full payment card numbers or payment authentication data on its own servers.

If you use our mobile applications, we may collect device and application information such as device ID, device model, operating system, app version, IP address, browser type, mobile carrier, device and application identifiers, usage data, crash reports, and performance information.

If you grant permission, we may collect precise or approximate location information in order to display nearby merchants, assign delivery drivers, enable live delivery tracking, improve dispatch, and support fraud prevention and security.

We may automatically collect technical and usage information such as IP address, browser and device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, page views, feature usage, app activity, timestamps, log data, and diagnostic information.

In limited cases, we may receive information from third-party service providers or operational partners as necessary to operate the platform, including payment processors, map and location providers, authentication providers, merchants, drivers, analytics providers, customer support tools, and fraud-prevention providers.

4. Sensitive Information

We do not intentionally request or process sensitive personal information unless such processing is specifically required for the provision of the Services, for legal compliance, for fraud or security prevention, or is otherwise provided voluntarily by a user in connection with a support request or legal matter.

Users should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive personal information through the platform or through general support channels unless specifically requested by us for a legitimate purpose.

5. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to create and manage accounts, authenticate users, process orders and payments, facilitate order preparation and delivery, provide customer service and support, communicate with users, improve the Services, monitor performance and usage trends, prevent fraud and abuse, ensure platform security, comply with legal obligations, exercise legal rights, send marketing communications where permitted by law, and enable location-based services and delivery logistics.

We process personal information only where we have an appropriate legal or operational basis to do so, including to provide the Services, fulfill contractual obligations, comply with law, protect legitimate interests, or act based on user consent where required.

6. When We Share Personal Information

To fulfill orders, we may share relevant information with merchants, restaurants, and independent delivery drivers, including the customer’s name, delivery address, phone number, order details, delivery instructions, and limited operational status information, solely to the extent necessary to prepare, fulfill, and deliver the order.

We may share personal information with third-party service providers that support the operation of the platform, such as providers of payment processing, cloud hosting, authentication, data storage, analytics, performance monitoring, customer support tools, order fulfillment infrastructure, mapping and geolocation services, website or application hosting, communications tools, and fraud-detection services.

We may disclose personal information where required or permitted by law, including to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests by authorities, investigate fraud or security incidents, enforce our terms and policies, or protect our rights, users, merchants, drivers, or the public.

We may share or transfer personal information in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, sale of assets, or transfer of all or part of our business.

We may allow authorized employees, contractors, customer-support personnel, operations staff, fraud-prevention personnel, finance or payment-review personnel, compliance personnel, and platform administrators to access personal information strictly on a need-to-know basis.

7. Cookies, Analytics, and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies, SDKs, pixels, log files, and similar technologies to operate and secure the Services, remember preferences, analyze usage and performance, improve functionality, prevent fraud and abuse, and support analytics and reporting.

We may also use third-party analytics services, including Google Analytics or similar tools, to understand how users interact with the Services. Users may control cookies through browser settings, subject to technical limitations.

If we implement a separate cookie notice, preference banner, or cookie-management tool in the future, that notice or tool will supplement this Privacy Policy.

8. Google Services and Maps Functionality

If the Services use Google Maps, Places, or other Google API services, certain information such as location data may be processed through those services to enable mapping, routing, address lookup, and related functionality.

Where applicable, our use of data received through Google APIs will be subject to the relevant Google API policies.

9. Location Data Usage

The platform may collect and use location information from users’ devices in order to show nearby merchants, support dispatch operations, assign drivers to orders, provide route and delivery tracking, confirm delivery status, and improve logistics and operational efficiency.

Location information may be shared with merchants, customers, or drivers only to the extent required to complete the order or operate the relevant feature.

10. Order Fulfillment Data Sharing

To enable merchants to prepare orders and drivers to complete deliveries, we may share necessary order-related personal information, including customer name, delivery address, phone number, order contents, merchant details, and driver-related status information.

This sharing is limited to what is reasonably necessary for order fulfillment, delivery coordination, customer support, and dispute resolution.

11. Marketing Communications and Direct Marketing

We may send administrative, transactional, and service-related communications such as account confirmations, order updates, receipts, support messages, security alerts, and important policy or service notices.

Where permitted by law, we may also send marketing or promotional communications by email, push notification, SMS, or other channels. Users may opt out of marketing communications at any time by using available unsubscribe tools, adjusting app settings, or contacting us directly.

Opting out of marketing communications will not prevent us from sending essential service-related messages concerning account activity, orders, security, payments, legal notices, or support interactions.

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including providing the Services, managing accounts, fulfilling orders, maintaining business and accounting records, resolving disputes, preventing fraud, complying with legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations, and enforcing our agreements.

13. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, organizational, and administrative measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

These measures may include access controls, restricted permissions, encryption in transit where appropriate, secure payment processing arrangements, monitoring and logging, vendor controls, internal security practices, and incident-management procedures.

No method of transmission over the internet and no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Accordingly, while we take reasonable steps to protect personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
